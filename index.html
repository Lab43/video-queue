Why does this exist?

I mostly built this website with teachers in mind, but it should be useful for anyone looking for an elegant way to share a collection of videos. Many teachers like to use videos in their classrooms, but all of the extra stuff video sharing websites put on the page can be distracting to students, especially YouTube's “Related Videos” which often aren't classroom-safe. I'm hoping this website will provide teachers with a simpler and safer way to show videos in the classroom.

What about ads?

Unfortunately there is no way to prevent YouTube from showing ads during videos. If a video has an ad on YouTube.com, it will have an ad here too. Sorry.
